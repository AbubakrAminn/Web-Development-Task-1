Welcome to the Hospital

Unity Hospital is a leading healthcare institution committed to providing high-quality medical services and compassionate care to our community. Located in the heart of the city, our state-of-the-art facility spans over 10 acres with a vast built-up area of 440,000 square feet. We take pride in our 300+ bed capacity, with the potential to expand to 400 beds, ensuring that we can accommodate a wide range of medical needs. As a tertiary care flagship unit of the Unity Hospitals Group, we specialize in various medical disciplines, including Cardiac Sciences, Neuro Sciences, Orthopaedics, Cancer, Emergency Medicine, and Solid Organ Transplants. With over 35 allied medical specialties under the same roof, we offer comprehensive healthcare services to our patients. Our commitment to holistic healthcare encompasses prevention, treatment, rehabilitation, and health education for patients, their families, and clients. Unity Hospital's dedicated team of healthcare professionals strives to make a positive impact on the lives of those we serve, prioritizing their well-being and recovery.
